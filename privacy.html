Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 20, 2026 · Last updated: April 20, 2026

This privacy policy describes how netfee.io (operated by Robot Arms LLC, Colorado) handles information when you use our calculators and content. We've tried to keep it short and plain-English.

What we don't collect

The calculators on netfee.io run entirely in your browser. Transaction amounts, card types, and any other values you enter are not transmitted to our servers. We don't store them, log them, or send them anywhere. Close the tab and they're gone.

What we do collect

Like most websites, we use standard web analytics to understand how people use netfee.io. This includes:

  • Page views — which pages you visit and when
  • Referrer — how you got here (search engine, link, direct)
  • Browser and device — generic info like "Chrome on iOS"
  • Approximate location — country/region level, derived from your IP address

We use this information in aggregate to improve the site. We do not attempt to identify individual visitors.

Cookies and advertising

netfee.io displays ads served by Google AdSense. Google and its partners use cookies to show you ads based on your visits to this and other sites on the internet.

  • Google uses cookies (including the DoubleClick DART cookie) to serve ads.
  • You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings.
  • You can opt out of a third-party vendor's use of cookies for personalized ads by visiting aboutads.info.

We do not control the specific ads shown or the data Google collects through its ad network. For Google's own privacy practices, see their Privacy & Terms.

Analytics

We may use Google Analytics 4 or a privacy-respecting alternative (such as Cloudflare Web Analytics or Plausible) to measure traffic. If we use Google Analytics, it sets cookies and sends data to Google. You can opt out using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Affiliate links

Some external links on netfee.io are affiliate links. If you click through and sign up with a payment processor or other service, we may earn a small commission at no additional cost to you. This does not influence the rates or information we publish. See our Affiliate Disclosure in the Terms of Use.

Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction (California, EU/UK, etc.), you may have rights to access, delete, or restrict processing of your personal information. Because we don't collect personally identifiable information directly, most requests relate to Google's ad and analytics cookies — those can be managed via Google's settings linked above.

If you have a specific privacy concern, email us at hello@netfee.io and we'll respond within 30 days.

Children

netfee.io is intended for a general business audience. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision. Material changes will be announced on the homepage.

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Email hello@netfee.io or visit the contact page.
